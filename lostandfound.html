Lost & Found

Left something on the bus? Here’s how to get it back.

Same Day

Ask the driver If you realize your item is missing while service is still running, you may ask a driver if they have seen your lost item. You may also ask to search their bus.
Do not ask drivers to radio Please do not ask drivers to radio around for your lost item. This is against company policy and interferes with safe bus operation.

Business Day(s) After

Items are collected after service ends After our buses finish service for the day, all found items are collected and delivered to the OCCT office or University Police Department’s Lost & Found by 5pm the following business day if found.
University Police Department (UPD) Lost & Found Basement of the Couper Administration Building
(607) 777-2393
Where your item ends up depends on its type
  • Valuables (wallets, electronics, keys, etc.) are turned over to UPD Lost & Found.
  • Personal items (scarves, hats, water bottles, etc.) are brought to the OCCT office at UUW B20.
If you’re unsure where your item was taken, call UPD at (607) 777-2393 or stop by the OCCT office (UUW B20) during business hours.

UPD Lost & Found

Basement of the Couper Administration Building on campus.

OCCT Office

Personal items (hats, scarves, water bottles) are held at our office.

University Union West, Room B20 (UUW B20)

Still need help?

If you can’t locate your item, contact our office directly.
