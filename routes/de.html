DE

Downtown Express

Late-night express Fri & Sat — route order changes after 1 AM

Route Stops & Schedule

No service this summer. The Downtown Express does not operate during Summer 2026 (5/25/26–8/7/26). Late Nite service returns when the fall semester begins.
The Downtown Express runs Friday and Saturday nights only, departing from University Union and serving Downtown State & Hawley Streets and UClub — University Plaza. After 1 AM the stop order flips: the bus goes Downtown first, then UClub on the way back to campus.

Passenger Notices

Route changes after 1 AM: Before 1 AM, UClub is served on the way out (before Downtown). After 1 AM the order reverses — Downtown first, UClub on the way back.
Fri & Sat only: The Downtown Express does not operate Sunday through Thursday.

Late Nite Service

  • Buses may be delayed due to incidents — allow extra wait time.
  • If you feel sick, notify the driver immediately. They can provide you with bags.
  • No standing on buses past midnight on Friday and Saturday nights.
  • Campus loading uses Union barricades; Downtown loading uses Hawley Street barricades.
  • Rule violations will result in expulsion from the bus.
  • Cleaning fee: Vomiting on a bus will result in a $150 biohazard fee charged to the responsible passenger.
