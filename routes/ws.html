Operating Times

Summer 2026 Service

Weekdays 8:15 AM – 7:40 PM
Weekends No OCCT Service

Late Nite Service

  • Buses may be delayed due to incidents — allow extra wait time.
  • If you feel sick, notify the driver immediately. They can provide you with bags.
  • No standing on buses past midnight on Friday and Saturday nights.
  • Campus loading uses Union barricades; Downtown loading uses Hawley Street barricades.
  • Rule violations will result in expulsion from the bus.
  • Cleaning fee: Vomiting on a bus will result in a $150 biohazard fee charged to the responsible passenger.
