OC

Oakdale Commons Shuttle

Loop service to Oakdale Commons Mall & Wegmans, and other shopping destinations

Route Stops & Schedule

No service this summer. The Oakdale Commons Shuttle does not operate during Summer 2026 (5/25/26–8/7/26). For shopping-area travel this summer, use the Vestal Shopping Shuttle or BC Transit. Service returns in the fall semester.
Departs Binghamton University, travels via NY-201 to Aldi, Oakdale Commons, and Wegmans, then returns to campus. All buses terminate at University Union.
