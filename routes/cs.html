CS

Campus Shuttle

Shuttle service serving all residential communities, parking lots, and key academic buildings — two loops operating around campus

Route Stops & Schedule

Summer 2026: The Campus Shuttle runs evenings only, Mon–Fri from 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM. Buses depart from Engineering and Mohawk on a fixed schedule (see below). No daytime continuous-loop service — for daytime travel use the RIU Shuttle. No service on weekends.
Important: Buses departing from the Engineering Building do not serve Mohawk, and buses departing from Mohawk do not serve the Engineering Building. These are separate loops — board at the correct stop for your destination.

No OCCT service on summer weekends. The Campus Shuttle does not operate Saturday or Sunday during summer service. For weekend transportation, please use BC Transit.

Inner Loop is suspended for summer 2026. The Inner Loop does not operate during summer service. This new loop will begin in the fall semester. For daytime campus and parking-lot travel this summer, use the RIU Shuttle.
The Inner Loop runs as a continuous loop, traveling around the Brain (inner campus road). When in service, it operates Monday–Friday during the day, with no fixed timetable.

Passenger Notices

Summer 2026: Evenings Only. CS operates Mon–Fri from 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM. No daytime continuous-loop service, no Inner Loop, no Late Nite, and no weekend service in summer.
Need daytime service? Use the RIU Shuttle for travel around campus residences, ITC, and UClub.
Engineering Bldg ↔ Mohawk (Outer Loop): These are separate loops. Buses from Engineering Building do not stop at Mohawk, and vice versa. Board at the correct stop.

Summer Daytime Alternative

The Campus Shuttle does not run daytime in summer. Use the RIU Shuttle. It serves residential communities, ITC, UClub, Meadows, and Hayes on a continuous loop, weekdays 8:00 AM–6:00 PM.
