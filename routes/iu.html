IU

ITC–UClub Shuttle

Service to UClub via the ITC

Route Stops & Schedule

No service this summer. The IU Shuttle does not operate during Summer 2026 (5/25/26–8/7/26). For travel to ITC, UClub, Meadows, and Hayes this summer, use the RIU Shuttle instead. Service returns in the fall semester.
Departs from Mohawk, travels via the Innovative Technologies Complex (ITC) to UClub and other living communities. The ITC-UClub route only runs on weekdays. Please view the UClub Route for additional service.
