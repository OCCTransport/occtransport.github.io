Lost an Item on the Bus?

If you lose your personal items while riding with us:


Same Day: You can ask a driver if they have seen your lost item and if you can search their bus for your lost item, but please do not ask them to radio around for your lost item as this is against company policy.


Day(s) After: After our buses finish service for the day, all found items will be collected and given to the University Police Department's Lost and Found.


UPD's Lost and Found is located at the basement of the Couper Administration Building on campus.
