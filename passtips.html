Passenger Tips

Everything you need to know for a smooth ride with OCC Transport.

Common Terms

Campus Shuttle
Smaller, green bus used for the Campus Shuttle route during regular-service weekdays and routes between semesters.
UDC
Binghamton’s University Downtown Center, located in Downtown Binghamton.
ITC
Binghamton’s Innovative Technologies Complex, located next to campus on Vestal Parkway.
HSC
Binghamton’s Health Sciences Campus, composed of the Pharmacy and Nursing schools in Johnson City.
Outbound
Route leaves campus and goes to the UDC (Downtown).
Inbound
Route leaves the UDC (Downtown) and goes to campus.
Shuttle
Route starts and ends on campus.
Late Nite / LN
OCCT’s service that runs from 12:00 AM – 3:15 AM on Fridays and Saturdays.
Terminus
Final stop along a route, or where the route changes to another route. This is often campus or the UDC.
Master PDF
A PDF document containing every route’s schedule for a certain semester or time period (e.g. breaks).

How to Ride the Bus

OCC Transport operates fixed-route, scheduled service built around class times and student needs. Weekday and weekend schedules differ: check the Routes & Schedules page before you ride.

Planning your trip
  • Check the routes and schedules page before heading out.
  • Arrive at your stop 5–10 minutes before the scheduled departure.
  • Wait only at designated bus stops.
  • Signal the driver to board when the bus arrives.
  • Have your BU ID ready to scan before boarding.
Boarding & safety
  • Enter through the front door only.
  • Do not stand forward of the white line at the front of the bus.
  • Move toward the rear of the bus after boarding to allow others to board.
  • Pull the cord to signal your exit request before your stop.
  • Exit through the rear door.
  • Do not cross in front of the bus after exiting.

Overcrowding

  • Expect crowding during bad weather: plan accordingly and allow extra time.
  • If you are standing, move rearward to allow additional passengers to board.
  • Standing forward of the floor line is prohibited by federal regulation.
  • If a bus is full, consider checking alternate routes or BC Transit as an alternative.

Guest Policy

  • One guest per BU ID is permitted on the bus.
  • Notify the driver that you have a guest when boarding.
  • The BU ID holder is responsible for their guest’s conduct for the duration of the visit.

Late Nite Service

  • Buses on late nite routes may be delayed due to incidents: allow extra wait time.
  • If you feel sick, notify the driver or on-board monitor immediately.
  • No standing on the buses past midnight on Friday and Saturday nights.
  • Campus loading uses Union barricades; Downtown loading uses Hawley Street barricades.
  • Rule violations will result in expulsion from the bus.
Cleaning fee notice: Vomiting on a bus will result in a cleaning fee charged to the responsible passenger.

Inclement Weather Emergency Procedure

  • When a RAVE emergency alert is issued, our emergency protocol is immediately activated.
  • Buses currently on campus will freeze in place, unload all passengers, and drivers will seek shelter.
  • Buses en route to campus will continue until they reach campus property, then follow the same procedure.
  • In extreme conditions where continuing is unsafe, drivers will pull over and unload passengers at the nearest building.

Tips & Policies

  • Don’t know where your stop is? Check out our website’s route pages for detailed photos & map pins!
  • Make sure to only board the bus through the front door.
  • Looking to go where OCCT doesn’t? Try BC Transit for free! More info at ridebctransit.com.
  • Got any more questions? Feel free to ask your driver or contact us!
