OCC Transport’s Training Program

Description

Our training program is a 6–8 week intensive program designed to develop qualified bus drivers. Participants earn a New York State Class B Commercial Driver’s License and receive a completion stipend after successfully completing the training program, getting hired, and passing a probationary period.

Program Overview

Our training program features 3-4 weekly driving sessions approximately 3-4 hours long, plus an additional 2-3 classroom hours each week. Flexible scheduling is available, however, our training program demands roughly 15-18 hours per week of time commitment both in and outside of scheduled training sessions. The NYS CDL Class B Road Test takes place towards the end of training. All training sessions are mandatory. All employees are required to work a minimum of approximately 15 hours per week.

Hiring Policy

OCC Transport is an Equal Opportunity Employer. Hiring decisions are based on demonstrated driving competency, rule compliance, and passage of the road test.

Other Positions

We also accept applications for the Lift (Paratransit Driver, no CDL Training) and Late Nite Event Staff on a rolling basis throughout the year. Please contact recruiting for more information.
