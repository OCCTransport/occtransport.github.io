Advertising

Reach over 60,000 weekly riders on OCC Transport’s bus fleet.

Rates

OCC Transport allows advertising on our blue and green buses for SA Organizations and Binghamton University departments. External groups may advertise if sponsored by an appropriate institutional entity.

SA Organizations
$75/ week
Student Association organizations
BU Departments
$85/ week
Binghamton University departments

Late Submission Fees

Submission Timing Late Fee
7 – 13 days before posting +$5.00
Less than 7 days before posting +$10.00

How to Submit an Ad

  1. 1
    Download and complete the request form appropriate for your group: SA Organization Form (PDF) or University Department Form (PDF).
  2. 2
    Email your advertisement along with the completed form to pr@occtransport.org.
  3. 3
    Await PR Coordinator approval. OCC Transport reserves the right to reject submissions due to space limitations or incomplete forms.
  4. 4
    Deliver 25 physical copies to the OCCT Office at University Union West, Room B20.
  5. 5
    Billing occurs the week after your advertisement is removed from the buses.

Requirements & Policies

  • External organizations must provide sponsorship documentation from an appropriate institutional entity.
  • Sponsored advertisements must clearly identify the sponsoring entity on the ad.
  • OCC Transport reserves the right to reject any advertisement for space limitations or incomplete submission forms.
  • Physical copies (25) must be delivered to UUW B20 prior to posting.
Billing is processed the week after removal.
