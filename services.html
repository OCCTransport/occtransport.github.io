Additional Services

Along with regular service, OCC Transport offers lift, charter, and advertising services.

Lift Service

Accessible transportation for students with disabilities

On-call wheelchair-accessible lift service for approved Binghamton University students, covering trips between residence and class. All vehicles feature wheelchair lifts with trained operators. Operates fall and spring semesters, 6:30 AM–10:00 PM.

Learn More

Charter Service

Off-campus charter trips for SA & BU-affiliated groups

Auxiliary bus service available to SA Organizations and Binghamton University affiliated groups for off-campus trips within a 100-mile radius. Blue buses seat 37; green shuttles seat 18. Minimum 4 weeks advance notice required.

Learn More & Request

Advertising

Reach over 60,000 weekly riders on our bus fleet

SA Organizations and Binghamton University departments are eligible to advertise on OCC Transport’s blue and green buses. Starting at $75/week for SA Organizations and $85/week for BU Departments.
